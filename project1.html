Kazakhstan official name-Republic of Kazakhstan (Kaz. Republic of Kazakhstan; Qazaqstan Respublikasy), — the state in the center of Eurasia, the largest part of which is related to Asia, the largest — to Europe. The area is 2,724,902 km2. The population, according to the State Statistics Committee on June 1, 2021, is 18,984,845 people. The population density is one of the lowest: more than 7 people per square kilometer. Capital - Nur-Sultan.
